# ERP Application

(ERP) Enterprise Resource Planning is a type of software that organizations use to manage day-to-day business activities such as accounting, procurement, project management, risk management and compliance, and supply chain operations. A complete ERP suite also includes enterprise performance management, software that helps plan, budget, predict, and report on an organization’s financial results.

## Features

- **Accounting**: Manage your general ledger, payables, receivables, fixed assets, and cash management, and gain a better financial insight into your business.
- **Financial Management**: Streamline your financial close, and get real-time visibility into the performance of your business.
- **Supply Chain Management**: Streamline your procurement, manufacturing, inventory, and warehouse management.
- **Project Management**: Keep projects on time, on budget, and reduce risk across your supply chain.
- **Customer Relationship Management**: Get a 360-degree view of your customers, including activity history, key contacts, customer communications, and internal account discussions.
- **Human Capital Management**: Streamline HR processes, such as organization management, workforce administration, time and attendance, and employee self-service.
- **Business Intelligence**: Get a real-time view of your financials, sales, and procurement data.
- **Reporting and Analytics**: Get real-time business intelligence from any device, and any data source.
- **Compliance and Risk Management**: Reduce operational risk, enforce compliance, and ensure data security with role-based access control.
- **Integration**: Seamlessly integrate with third-party solutions for shipping, e-commerce, and customer relationship management.
